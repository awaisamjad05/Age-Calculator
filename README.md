# ⏳ AG-Tech Chronos — Premium Age & Cosmic Portal

Chronos is a handcrafted, state-of-the-art, single-page age dashboard built using vanilla HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. It operates with zero external AI dependencies, relying entirely on precise astronomical and calendar mathematics to deliver an immersive, premium user experience.

## ✨ Premium Features

* **Real-Time Chronological Ticking:** Watch your precise age increment down to the exact second in real time.
* **Multitasking Profiles:** Seamlessly create, edit, and delete multiple age profiles (for family, friends, or pets) stored locally inside your browser via `localStorage`.
* **Accent Customization:** Personalize your dashboard with 5 vibrant ambient themes (**Indigo, Rose, Emerald, Amber, Cyan**) featuring active neon glow reflections.
* **Progress Circle Countdown:** A gorgeous, animated circular countdown indicating exactly how many days, hours, minutes, and seconds remain until your next birthday celebration.
* **Life Journey Progress:** A fluid visual progress bar mapping out your lifecycle (optimized for an 80-year lifespan) complete with active and completed milestones.
* **Zodiac & Astro Signposts:** Automatically calculates and charts your **Western Zodiac** (with elements and date ranges) and **Chinese Zodiac** (with cycle animals and elements).
* **Biological Milestones:** Real-time calculated estimates of your total heartbeats taken, breaths inhaled, and hours slept, alongside historical folklore insights based on the specific day of the week you were born.
* **Cosmic Planet Explorer:** Discover your exact age scaled to **Martian, Venusian, or Jovian** years, along with live countdowns for your upcoming planetary birthdays.
* **Time Travel Simulator:**
  * **Age Simulator:** Project your exact age onto any specific calendar date far into the future.
  * **Milestone Calculator:** Input major milestones (e.g., reaching 10,000 days lived or 1 billion seconds lived) to pinpoint the exact day they will be achieved.
* **Celebration Effects:** Dynamic visual feedback featuring built-in canvas-confetti bursts upon profile creation and birthday countdown completions.
